Clothing

Screen Printing

Screen printing is a versatile and widely-used method for fabric customization, ideal for bold and vibrant designs. This technique involves creating a stencil (or screen) and using it to apply layers of ink directly onto the fabric. The result is a durable, high-quality print with vivid colors that resist fading, even after multiple washes. Screen printing is perfect for large orders and works best on cotton fabrics or blends. It is particularly effective for designs with limited colors and is a cost-effective option for bulk printing.

Cotton / Cotton & Polyster

Cotton fabric is known for its softness, breathability, and natural absorbency, making it an ideal choice for various garments. Available as 100% cotton or blended with polyester for added durability and reduced shrinkage, it offers excellent comfort and ease of care and is available in a wide range of GSMs. We use this versatile fabric specifically for crafting T-shirts and karate suits. Its breathability and flexibility make it perfect for everyday wear in T-shirts, while its strength and softness provide the necessary comfort and movement for karate suits.

Direct-to-Film (DTF) Printing

Direct-to-Film (DTF) printing is a cutting-edge technology that offers exceptional flexibility and detail in fabric customization. In this process, the design is printed onto a special transfer film, which is then heat-pressed onto the fabric. DTF printing works on a wide range of materials, including cotton, polyester, and blends, making it an excellent choice for creating intricate designs with fine details. The prints are durable, stretch-resistant, and maintain their vibrancy over time. DTF is particularly useful for smaller orders or designs that require high detail and full-color prints.

Direct-to-Garment (DTG) Printing

Direct-to-Garment (DTG) printing is a digital printing method that allows for high-resolution, full-color designs to be applied directly to the fabric. This process is ideal for intricate designs with multiple colors or photographic elements. DTG printing works best on cotton fabrics and provides a soft, breathable print that integrates seamlessly with the material. It is an excellent option for custom orders where each item has a unique design or for small runs. DTG allows for quick turnaround times and produces prints with a high level of detail and color accuracy.

Sublimation Printing

Sublimation printing is a premium customization technique that produces vibrant, full-color designs that are permanently embedded into the fabric. This process involves printing the design onto a special sublimation paper and then using heat to transfer the ink into the fabric's fibers. Sublimation works best on polyester fabrics or blends and is ideal for creating all-over prints that cover the entire garment. The prints are highly durable, resist fading, and do not crack or peel. Sublimation is perfect for sportswear, activewear, and any fabric that benefits from lightweight, breathable, and vibrant designs.

Embroidery

Embroidery is a timeless customization option that adds a touch of elegance and durability to fabric. This technique involves stitching the design directly onto the fabric using colored threads, creating a textured, raised effect. Embroidery is ideal for logos, monograms, and other designs where a sophisticated and long-lasting finish is desired. It works well on a variety of fabrics, including cotton, polyester, and even thicker materials like jackets and caps. While embroidery is often used for smaller designs, it adds a professional and high-quality touch to any garment.

Embroidered Patches

As part of our embroidery services, we also offer custom embroidered patches. These patches can be sewn or heat-applied onto garments, offering a versatile and removable option for branding and personalization. Embroidered patches are perfect for uniforms, team apparel, or promotional items. They provide a textured, detailed look with the durability and quality of traditional embroidery, but with the added flexibility of being easily applied to various fabrics and garments.

Protective Gear & Training Equipment

Screen Printing

Screen printing is a popular and effective method for customizing martial arts gear and equipment made from PU and leather. This technique involves applying layers of ink directly onto the surface, allowing for bold and vibrant designs with excellent durability. Screen printing is ideal for logos, brand names, and other designs that require sharp, eye-catching colors. It works well on PU materials and certain types of treated leather, providing a high-quality finish that resists wear and tear, making it a reliable option for items like gloves, pads, and protective vests.

Embossing

Embossing is a high-end customization technique that creates a raised, 3D effect on PU and leather surfaces by pressing a design into the material. This method is perfect for adding a sophisticated and tactile element to martial arts protective gear, such as gloves, shin guards, and headgear. Embossing works best for logos, brand names, or subtle design elements, giving the gear a professional and premium appearance. The raised design not only adds texture but also enhances the visual appeal, making it a preferred choice for premium or luxury products.

Debossing

Debossing is the opposite of embossing, where the design is pressed into the material to create an indented effect. This technique provides a sleek and understated look that works particularly well on leather and PU materials. Debossing is ideal for subtle branding or adding intricate details to gear like belts, gloves, and focus pads. It offers a clean and durable finish, giving the product a refined, minimalist aesthetic while ensuring the design remains long-lasting even under heavy use.

Rubber Logos

Rubber logos are a durable and flexible customization option perfect for martial arts gear that sees heavy wear and tear. These logos are made from molded rubber and can be affixed to PU and leather surfaces through sewing or adhesive methods. Rubber logos provide a three-dimensional, textured appearance that stands out on gear like gloves, shin guards, and headgear. This method is ideal for bold branding or logos that need to remain prominent and durable over time. The flexibility and toughness of rubber logos make them especially suitable for products exposed to frequent impact and movement.

Woven Labels

Woven labels are a versatile and durable customization option for martial arts gear, adding a professional branding touch. These labels are made of tightly woven threads that create detailed logos or text, and can be sewn onto PU and leather products. Woven labels work particularly well for items like gloves, shin guards, and uniforms, where a soft but durable branding element is required. They offer a clean, precise finish that maintains its appearance over time, even with regular use.
